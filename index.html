About Me

I am a Computer Vision and Robotics Engineer with a strong focus on 3D perception, multi-view systems, and real-world AI pipelines.

My work combines geometric reasoning, deep learning, and system-level engineering to build scalable and efficient solutions. I have extensive experience designing multi-camera systems, calibration pipelines, and GPU-accelerated processing using PyTorch and CUDA.

I enjoy working on problems that connect perception with real-world systems, such as tracking, reconstruction, and sensor fusion.
